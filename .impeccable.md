# Impeccable — clinical-guidelines

Project-specific notes for AI-assisted design and frontend work.

## Design Context

### Users

- **Audiences**: **Hospital physicians** (primary for day-to-day clinical workflow) and **patients** (生病患者)—content and tone should distinguish clinician tools vs. patient-facing surfaces when both exist.
- **Physician context**: Chinese-language workflow—**Agent 医学问答** with patient context, **指南数据库**, **病例库** / patient journey; desktop-first shell; clarity and trust over novelty.
- **Jobs to be done**: Find guideline logic, align with a patient’s journey/timeline, and use the agent for node- or text-level explanations—without diluting pathway accuracy.

### Brand Personality

- **Overall voice**: **Intelligent assistant**—approachable, efficient help (similar in spirit to doctor-facing medical AI products).
- **Pathway & guideline surfaces**: **Authoritative guideline** tone—诊疗路径、指南树、时间线等模块应像可核对来源的临床路径，少营销感、少模糊表述。
- **3-word personality**: **Trustworthy · Clear · Capable** — serious medical product, not consumer lifestyle UI.
- **Emotional goals**: Confidence and calm under information density; patients need reassurance without overselling cures.

### Aesthetic Direction

- **Visual tone (from code)**: **Light content area** with **dark sidebar** (`#1a1a1a`) as the **long-term fixed pattern**; warm/peach brand tints (`--brand-bg: #FAECE7`), **brand teal** `--brand: #2A8075`, blues/greens for semantics. Titles: **Georgia**; body: **system-ui**; patient journey (`.pj-v4`): system stack + **Noto Sans SC** for CJK.
- **Theme policy**: Do **not** align the app shell or feature UI with the **purple accent** in root `src/index.css`—that file is for legacy/global defaults; **platform.css** and feature CSS own product visual language. No requirement to unify with `index.css` dark/purple tokens unless explicitly refactoring globals later.
- **References** (team alignment):
  - [NCCN Clinical Practice Guidelines in Oncology](https://guidelines.nccn.org/) — structure, hierarchy, and “guideline reading” clarity for oncology-style pathways.
  - [氢离子 · 阿里健康医学 AI（医生端）](https://www.ali-doctor.com/) — doctor-focused medical AI assistant: search, guidelines, reading, Q&A—**smart assistant** positioning in a Chinese hospital context.
- **Anti-references**: Generic “AI SaaS” purple gradients, glassmorphism on primary chrome, decorative motion that slows reading of clinical content.

### Design Principles

1. **Clinical readability first**: Dense tables, timelines, and guideline trees must scan quickly; hierarchy beats decoration.
2. **Brand consistency**: Prefer existing tokens (`--brand`, `--pj-selected`, semantic blues/greens) before introducing new palettes—**not** the purple accent from `index.css` for product surfaces.
3. **Bilingual-safe typography**: Chinese + Latin mixed UI—keep line-height and font stacks predictable (system / Noto Sans SC where scoped).
4. **Trust through restraint**: Micro-interactions and “delight” are secondary to accurate labels, clear states, and error copy; **pathway views** stay guideline-serious.
5. **Accessible by default**: Interactive targets and contrast on light content areas should meet common clinical workstation expectations; respect `prefers-reduced-motion` when adding motion.

### Accessibility & inclusion

- **Default**: Aim for **WCAG 2.1 AA** on new work unless a screen specifies otherwise.
- **Motion**: Honor `prefers-reduced-motion` for non-essential animation.
- **Color**: Do not rely on color alone for critical distinctions (pair with text/icons).
- **Hospital policy**: Update this section if a specific institution mandates stricter checks.
